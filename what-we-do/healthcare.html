What We Do

Driving Healthcare Transformation

Technology has dramatically changed the landscape of the Healthcare industry and continues to disrupt traditional methods of care and service. Organizations that have adopted Digital Transformation strategies have seen major growth in revenue, patient/client satisfaction, lower readmittance rates, etc. Here at BCL, transformation is in our DNA, and we excel in analyzing, defining, and implementing game-changing solutions for our clients.

From Startup organizations to the largest Healthcare providers in the world, BCL has the experience and expertise to deliver best-in-class software solutions on time and on budget.

Digital Trends in Healthcare

COVID-19 has accelerated the need for Digital Transformation across all industries, however, Healthcare faces a unique challenge. Implementing current and emerging technologies to better serve patients and clients is crucial, but must be delivered strategically and correctly. Our team of over 350 digital experts can assist in leveraging the appropriate technologies to achieve your current goals, and stay competitive in the current business environment.

On-Demand & Virtual Services
On-Demand & Virtual Services
People today demand instant gratification. Tele-Health or In-Home services are exploding, and have been accelerated by Covid-19. The ability for organizations to engage with patients/clients virtually is critical to remain competitive, while increasing satisfaction and scalability.

Mobile Apps
Mobile Apps
The ability for patients/clients to access their records, or quickly find answers is critical for organizations to remain competitive. Though Apps have been around for years, many Healthcare organizations have not fully embraced their potential, or have been reactive with implementation. Patients and clients demand this functionality, and organizations must adopt immediately to stay competitive.
02/06
Wearables & IoT
Wearables & IoT
Wearable technology continues to push the boundaries of Healthcare. From predictive diagnosis, maintenance, tracking, and analytics, Healthcare providers and organizations now have new visibility into patients and clients, to ensure best possible treatments & outcomes.
03/06
Big Data
Big Data
Technology has given Healthcare providers and organizations access to a tremendous amount of data on their patients and clients. Most of this was the result of years of paper processes. What to do with all of this information? A defined Big-Data strategy can not only simplify business practices, but find commonalities, predictive analytics, and insights that can reinvent the way care is delivered.
04/06
Enhanced Security & Blockchain
Enhanced Security & Blockchain
With Data comes security. The more information that is digitized, demands the latest and greatest security protocols to ensure patient data remains confidential, and industry regulations are met.
05/06
AI & Machine Learning
AI & Machine Learning
Machine Learning is a well-studied discipline extensively used in many industries. In healthcare it is applied to improve patient care, chronic disease management, hospital administration, and supply chain efficiencies.

Our Process

Discover

What are your goals? Where do you want to go? What pain points do you currently have?

Analyze

We then review your current Digital Strategy, mobile footprint, and legacy systems to see what is working, and what changes would need to be made.

Identify

Our team will then propose several different options for not only how to achieve your stated goals, but what else you can be doing that you may not have even thought of.

Design

Once a strategy is identified, our team of Analysts, Designers, UI/UX Experts, and Architects, will design the solution on the most current and scalable tech-stack.

Develop

Our team of experienced, Senior Developers will then develop the system in an agile fashion, ensuring complete transparency throughout the build.

Deploy & Enhance

After rigorous QA testing, we will deploy your solution to the marketplace. We will then focus on supporting and discussing any possible enhancements, or new features with your team.
