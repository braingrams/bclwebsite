02 Wearables Today

Why limit yourself only to a smartphone? Having opened a completely new section in the market not so long ago, wearables bring entirely new possibilities and ways of interactions to users. Wearable technology is becoming more and more advanced than hand-held technology as it provides a wide range of new sensory and scanning features.

01
Smart Watches
They are functional and portable, sit comfortably on your wrist and don’t interfere with your sport and daily activities. They make it easier to see and track the information you need.
02
Fitness Trackers
Being constantly upgraded, as of today, the fitness trackers not just monitor the user’s activity and vitals, but also guide their workouts and track their runs.
03
Smart Glasses
Experts believe the next modern ear-worn devices will come packed with fitness tracking functionalities, medical monitoring devices, and even hearing aids.
04
Body Sensors
With a wearable body sensor you can feel more in control of your health. Just let the app keep track of all your vital metrics and get back to you with clear health reports.
05
VR Headsets
Head-mounted VR tech certainly promises a whole panorama of perspectives. We believe it will get a boost in the coming years thanks to the advent of quality content.

03 Wearables Tomorrow

Wondering about upcoming wearable technology trends?

By 2022, the number of connected wearable devices is going to reach a staggering 1.1 billion. The global wearables market is going to grow to a whopping 302.3 million units by the year 2023. Obviously, in the coming years the world expects dramatic improvements in the following life spheres due to cutting-edge wearable technologies.
