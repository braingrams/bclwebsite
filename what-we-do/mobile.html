02Recipe of the Mobile Application

It is pretty simple — just take a couple of analysts, a pinch of designers, a tablespoon of developers and 100 ml of testers.

Scope Definition

An idea is what every project starts with! We work together to obtain sufficient project details, clarify and prioritize your requirements, and prepare the project roadmap.

Analysis & Design

You’ll see how a distinctive look and feel of your project is materializing as we establish user flows and lay out wireframes and mockups.

Customer Reviews

At each step of analysis and design phase, we’re willing to perform several review and feedback sessions to make sure that we’ve captured all subtleties.

Application Development

The development kicks off! We use a sprint-based approach, with planning and demo meetings every other week, that gives you a great visibility and control over the scope of the project.

Quality Assurance

Our thorough and in-depth QA process includes functional and exploratory testing. If applicable, we also use automated testing tools.

Deployment

When your solution is ready, we’ll help you deploy it and handle the tricky app submission process. We will continue working on your version roadmap, or switch to a support framework.
