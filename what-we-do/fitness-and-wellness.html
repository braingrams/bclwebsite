Popular Trends

When it comes to maintaining a healthy lifestyle, people tend to rely on technological solutions and monitor health and wellness in real time. The boost in wellness & fitness app technologies has allowed personal trainers, dietitians, and nutritionists to deliver services promptly and efficiently to their clients.

Tracking Progress

Progress makes users confident and motivated. An ability to see their fitness milestones and filter through progress on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis — that’s what features a quality wellness app.

Fitness Plan

Every user needs a personal fitness plan depending on their goals and current state of health. Providing them with an ability to build such a plan is one of our primary missions.

Calorie Counter

It’s great to have the ability to track calories as the user goes about their day. This is what really helps them feel in control and ensure they’re staying on track with nutrition and planning.

Nutrition Plan

“You are what you eat” a proverb says. In order to work the way towards health, we focus today on nutrition and eating well. And sticking to an eating plan is crucial to achieving a specific weight goal of course.

Video Workouts

Text explanations may be hard to follow. Video performance of a workout can help the user do a better job. The workouts could consist of a series of clips with trainers properly performing exercise movements.

Years of successful work

Projects Completed

In the last few years BCL has delivered numerous applications for the web, mobile and wearable devices in the Fitness & Wellness industry. Here we introduce for your consideration some of our high-profile projects.

Fitness Trainer App

This is one of the most popular applications developed by BCL. It’s used by millions of people and owes its popularity to great personalization and user experience. The app supports data sync with other fitness apps and Chromecast.

Tonal

Tonal is the world’s most intelligent fitness system featured by a multi-track video & sound playback. The immersive live experience is procured due to the nearly real-time data capture and visualization and deep integration with custom hardware and firmware components.

FitnessBank

FitnessBank is a division of Affinity Bank which aims to provide increased interest rates to clients who live an active lifestyle, and we’re pleased to be a part of this idea implementation with our jointly developed mobile application — FitnessBank Step Tracker App.

Among BCL Development work experience records is Fitbit — one of the world’s leading apps for health and fitness. It’s used by more than 20,000,000 people as a companion app for wearable activity trackers, smart scales, and other devices, and is available on a variety of platforms: Android, iOS, Windows, and web. The Fitbit app is a perfect tool for reaching one’s fitness goals: it features in-depth tracking and analysis of activity, workouts, sleep, nutrition, and stress levels, as well as endless options to stay motivated and connected to your fitness community.
