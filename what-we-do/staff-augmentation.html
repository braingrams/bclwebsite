Our Process

Idea
Idea
An idea is what every project starts with. Once you have the idea — we’ll do our best to learn about the goals you’re looking to reach with the new software. We will also introduce the BCL team so that we could get to know each other and see if we’re a good match.

Requirements Analysis
Requirements Analysis
Then we work closely together: discuss and clarify your requirements, prioritize functionality and prepare the project roadmap to ensure that key app features are delivered first.
02/06
Design
Design
That’s the fun part! In this stage you’ll see the project materializing as we lay out wireframes and mockups. You always have the final say in creating a distinctive look and feel for your solution.
03/06
Development
Development
We use a sprint-based approach for development of your project. The work is planned on a bi-weekly basis and the customer has a great visibility and control over the scope of each sprint. At the end of the sprint we deliver a build and schedule a dedicated demo meeting.
